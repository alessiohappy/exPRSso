exPRSso, what else?

"exPRSso" is an application that allows you to reproduce an existing, already calculated Polygenic Risk Score (PRS) in your cohort/data, such as those stored in PGScatalog.

This app leverages on published PRS summaries for a given trait to compute the score in your data, allowing you to reproduce the PRS with ease. All you need to get started is three simple files: the published PRS summary, and your genetic data which should be reported in a specific format (.ped and .map files).

The objective of exPRSso is to test the reproducibility of published PRSs. This help us assess how well they predict a specific trait in another cohort. However, keep in mind that the ethnicity of your population should be the same of the cohort in which the original PRS was calculated. A PRS with good generalizability across different cohorts is a measure that properly represents the genetic susceptibility underlying the risk of a particular trait. Isn't that cool.
